About Me

Turning AI ideas into systems that actually work.

Hi, I'm Fatih, an ICT student at Fontys University of Applied Sciences in Eindhoven. I started as a full-stack developer, building a solid foundation in web and backend development, before making a full switch to AI in my second year and never looked back.

In Machine Learning, I've trained and validated models from scratch using Python, Scikit-learn and TensorFlow, with hands-on experience in model comparison, evaluation metrics and real-world validation. In Deep Learning, I've built CNN-based classifiers, worked with object detection using YOLOv8, applied OCR pipelines for text extraction and used Grad-CAM explainability to interpret model decisions.

On the Generative AI side, I've worked with large language models through OpenAI, Anthropic and OpenRouter APIs, built LLM evaluation pipelines and developed real benchmarks to measure model reliability. I've also worked in depth on RAG strategies, benchmarking over ten retrieval approaches with ChromaDB and local LLMs as part of a privacy-first group project. I'm currently working on fine-tuning a lightweight LLM for low-resource language writing assistance.

IBCS Title Compliance Detection

Team Project · Client: JUGO

An AI application developed for JUGO to analyze dashboard screenshots and check whether titles follow IBCS Rule B. The system uses YOLOv8 to detect title areas, RapidOCR to extract the text, and SmolLM to generate feedback and improved title suggestions. It evaluates the Who–What–When structure and identifies issues such as missing units, missing time periods, unclear wording, or incorrect placement.
