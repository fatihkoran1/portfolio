Fruit Quality Classifier

ML & Deep Learning · Fontys

An ML and Deep Learning project that assesses banana shelf quality from a single image, classifying it as Fresh, Eatable, Near Expiry, or Spoiled. IBCS Title Compliance Detection

Team Project · Client: JUGO

An AI application developed for JUGO to analyze dashboard screenshots and check whether titles follow IBCS Rule B. The system uses YOLOv8 to detect title areas, RapidOCR to extract the text, and SmolLM to generate feedback and improved title suggestions. It evaluates the Who–What–When structure and identifies issues such as missing units, missing time periods, unclear wording, or incorrect placement.
